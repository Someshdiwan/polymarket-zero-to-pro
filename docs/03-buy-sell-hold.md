# 🔄 Buy, Sell & Hold — Trading Mechanics

> Everything you need to know about executing trades on Polymarket from clicking Buy to getting your money back.

---

## The 3 Actions You Can Take

```
BUY   → Enter a position (bet on outcome)
SELL  → Exit early (take profit or cut loss)
HOLD  → Do nothing, wait for resolution
```

---

## Part 1 : BUYING

### How to Buy Step by Step
```
Step 1 → Find a market you understand
Step 2 → Choose YES or NO
Step 3 → Enter amount ($1, $2, $5 etc)
Step 4 → Check "To Win" amount
Step 5 → Click Buy
Step 6 → Shares appear in Positions tab ✅
Step 7 → Money deducted from Cash balance
```

### What Happens After Buying
```
Your shares are locked in your wallet
Price can go up or down
Your share count NEVER changes
Your max payout NEVER changes
Only current sell value changes
```

### Market Order vs Limit Order
```
Market Order  = Buy immediately at current price
                Fast but price may slip slightly

Limit Order   = Set your own price
                Waits until someone sells at your price
                Better price but may not fill
```

> 💡 Beginners → always use Market Order

### Before You Buy Checklist
```
✅ Do I understand this market?
✅ Do I have an edge (information advantage)?
✅ Is volume high enough? ($50K+)
✅ Is price in 5-50¢ range? (avoid 99¢ markets)
✅ Am I betting max 20% of my balance?
✅ Can I afford to lose this amount completely?
```

## Part 2 : SELLING (Early Exit)

### How to Sell Step by Step
```
Step 1 → Go to Portfolio → Positions tab
Step 2 → Find your open position
Step 3 → Click "Sell" button
Step 4 → Move slider (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
Step 5 → Check "Receive" amount
Step 6 → Click "Cash Out $X.XX"
Step 7 → Money back in Cash balance instantly ✅
```

### Sell Slider Options
```
25%  → Sell quarter of your shares
50%  → Sell half (lock some profit, keep some)
75%  → Sell most, keep small position
100% → Sell everything, fully exit
```

### When You See Profit Act Fast!
```
Profit showing 100%+?
→ Don't think
→ Don't wait
→ Click Sell immediately 🚨

Real example from Day 1:
Bought UP at 4¢ with $1
Showed +462% profit ($5.62)
Didn't sell fast enough → lost it all

Lesson: High profit = sell first, think later
```

### Sell Price Calculation
```
You own 357 NO shares
Current NO price = 10¢

Sell value = 357 × 0.10 = $35.70
You get    = $35.70 (minus tiny fee)
Profit     = $35.70 - $10 (invested) = +$25.70
```

## Part 3 : HOLDING

### When to Hold
```
✅ You are confident in the outcome
✅ Market hasn't resolved yet
✅ Current sell value is less than potential payout
✅ News supports your position
✅ Enough time remaining
```

### When NOT to Hold
```
❌ You are up 100%+ (sell instead)
❌ News strongly goes against your bet
❌ Market resolving in next few minutes
❌ You need the money urgently
❌ You're holding because of emotion not logic
```

### The Hold Trap 🪤
```
Common mistake:
See $300 potential payout
Current value = $50
Greed kicks in → hold for $300
Market resolves wrong
Get $0 instead of $50

Rule: A bird in hand > two in the bush
```

## Part 4 All Scenarios with Real Numbers

### Setup
```
You buy NO at 2¢
Amount = $10
Shares = $10 ÷ 0.02 = 500 shares
To win = $500
```

### Scenario A : Price Rises, You Sell Early ✅
```
NO price rises from 2¢ → 10¢
Current value = 500 × 0.10 = $50
You SELL → get $50
Profit = $50 - $10 = +$40 (+400%)

You gave up potential $500
But locked in $40 safely ✅
```

### Scenario B : Price Drops, You Hold 😰
```
NO price drops from 2¢ → 0.5¢
Current value = 500 × 0.005 = $2.50
You HOLD (don't panic sell)

If NO wins at resolution → still get $500 ✅
If YES wins at resolution → get $0 ❌

Price drop doesn't matter if you hold to end
```

### Scenario C : Price Drops, You Panic Sell ❌
```
NO price drops from 2¢ → 0.5¢
Current value = $2.50
You SELL in panic → get $2.50
Loss = $2.50 - $10 = -$7.50

Then market resolves NO wins
You would have got $500
Panic cost you $497.50
```

### Scenario D : Hold to Resolution, Win ✅
```
You hold all 500 shares
Market resolves → NO wins
500 × $1 = $500 auto credited
Profit = $500 - $10 = +$490 (+4900%)
No action needed ✅
```

### Scenario E : Hold to Resolution, Lose ❌
```
You hold all 500 shares
Market resolves → YES wins
500 × $0 = $0
Loss = -$10 (total investment gone)
No action needed (nothing to do)
```

### Scenario F : Partial Sell Strategy 🧠
```
NO price rises from 2¢ → 10¢
Current value = $50

Sell 50% → get $25 (locked profit)
Hold 50% → 250 shares still active

If NO wins → 250 × $1 = $250 more
Total = $25 + $250 = $275

Best of both worlds ✅
```

## Part 7 : Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1 : Not Selling at 100%+ Profit
```
❌ "Let me wait for even more profit"
✅ Sell immediately at 100%+ always
```

### Mistake 2 : Panic Selling at Loss
```
❌ Price dropped 50% → panic sell
✅ Check news first → if no change → hold
```

### Mistake 3 : Averaging Down
```
❌ Lost $5 → bet $10 more to recover
✅ Each bet is independent, never chase
```

### Mistake 4 : Holding Too Long
```
❌ Refusing to sell because "it might go higher"
✅ Take profit at reasonable levels
```

### Mistake 5 : Forgetting to Check Positions
```
❌ Winning position sits unchecked for days
✅ Check Positions tab every day
```

## Part 8 : Auto Resolve vs Manual Sell

| | Auto Resolve | Manual Sell |
|---|---|---|
| When | After market deadline | Anytime before deadline |
| Action needed | Nothing ✅ | Click Sell |
| Price you get | $1 per share (if win) | Current market price |
| Works offline | Yes ✅ | No |
| Best for | Confident positions | Taking early profit |
