# 🎯 What is Polymarket?

> Polymarket is the world's largest decentralized prediction market a financial platform where probabilities are priced by the market, not dictated by a bookmaker.

---

## Simple Definition

```
Polymarket = A platform where you bet YES or NO
             on real world events using crypto
             
If you are right → You win money
If you are wrong → You lose your bet
```

---

## Real World Example

```
Question: "Will US strike Iran on March 2, 2026?"

Market says:
→ YES = 97¢  (97% chance it happens)
→ NO  = 3¢   (3% chance it doesn't)

You buy NO with $10
→ If NO strike happens = You win $333
→ If strike happens    = You lose $10
```

---

## How is it Different from Normal Betting?

| Feature | Normal Betting | Polymarket |
|---|---|---|
| Who sets odds | Bookmaker | Crowd (you!) |
| Bias | House always wins | No house edge |
| Topics | Sports only | Anything |
| Transparency | Hidden | 100% public |
| Payout | Fixed by bookie | Dynamic |
| Currency | INR/USD | USDC crypto |

---

## How Does Pricing Work?

```
Price = Probability

10¢ = market thinks 10% chance
50¢ = market thinks 50% chance  
99¢ = market thinks 99% chance

Price is set by traders like YOU buying and selling
More people buy YES → YES price goes up
More people buy NO  → NO price goes up
```

### Example
```
100 traders think Iran will be struck
→ They buy YES
→ YES price rises to 97¢
→ Only 3% chance left for NO = 3¢
```

---

## The Core Mechanic is Shares

```
Each share is worth exactly $1 if you win
Each share is worth exactly $0 if you lose

You buy shares cheap → hope to sell or win at $1
```

### Share Math
```
Price = 10¢
You invest $1
Shares = $1 ÷ 0.10 = 10 shares

If you WIN  → 10 shares × $1 = $10  ✅
If you LOSE → 10 shares × $0 = $0   ❌
Profit = $10 - $1 = $9
```

## How Does Resolution Work?

```
Market has a deadline (e.g. March 2, 2026)
          ↓
Deadline passes
          ↓
Polymarket checks real world outcome
(Uses trusted data sources, news, APIs)
          ↓
Winners get $1 per share automatically
          ↓
Money appears in your Cash balance
          ↓
No action needed from you ✅
```

## Polymarket vs Stock Market

| Feature | Stock Market | Polymarket |
|---|---|---|
| What you trade | Company shares | Event outcomes |
| Time horizon | Years | Hours to months |
| Minimum bet | Varies | $1 |
| Max profit | Unlimited | Fixed at $1/share |

## Key Terms Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **YES share** | Bet that event HAPPENS |
| **NO share** | Bet that event DOES NOT happen |
| **Price** | Probability in cents (50¢ = 50%) |
| **Shares** | How many units you own |
| **Resolution** | When market officially closes |
| **USDC** | Stable crypto = always $1 |
| **Liquidity** | How much money is in market |
| **Volume** | Total traded in market |
| **Edge** | Your advantage over market price |
